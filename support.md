## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I take a photo with a mask?
Select a mask from the carousel at the bottom of the screen, then tap the capture button to take a photo. Your photo will be saved to your photo library.

### How do I record a video?
Long-press the capture button to start recording a video with your AR mask applied. Release to stop recording. The video will be saved to your photo library.

### How do I switch masks?
Swipe through the mask carousel at the bottom of the screen and tap on any mask to apply it instantly.

### The AR masks aren't showing up. What should I do?
Make sure you've granted camera permission in Settings > HeroMask AR > Camera. AR face tracking requires a device with a TrueDepth camera (iPhone X or later). Ensure your face is well-lit and clearly visible to the camera.

### How do I flip the camera?
Tap the camera rotate button next to the capture button to switch between front and rear cameras.

### Is my data private?
Yes. All photos and videos are saved directly to your device. We do not collect any personal information, and no data is sent to external servers. See our [Privacy Policy](privacy) for full details.

### Which devices are supported?
HeroMask AR requires an iPhone or iPad with a TrueDepth camera running iOS 17.0 or later.
